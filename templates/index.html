Crazy Filter

Made by Chris Williams, Dan Cohen, and Daniel Padawer

What is it?

Crazy Filter pulls random comments from The New York Times' Community API and uses a Bayes classifier to classify them as crazy or sane. Crazy comments are highlighted as red Sane comments are highlighted as blue.
